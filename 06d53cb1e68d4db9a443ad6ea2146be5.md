> dirb http://www.megacorpone.com -r -z 10

> dirb http://target.com -a "Mozilla/5.0 (X11; Linux x86_64) AppleWebKit/537.36 (KHTML, like Gecko) Chrome/51.0.2704.106 Safari/537.36"
